About Us

Guelph Bhutanese Community (GBC) History

Guelph Bhutanese Community is a non-profit organization of Nepali-Speaking Bhuatnese people living in Guelph. Organization was officially registered in federal government on February 2016. The organization is run by community volunteers providing services in sports, cultural programs, and much more

From various Canadian provinces and cities Nepali speaking Bhutanese started to relocate in Guelph in since the early 2014. The very first Bhutanese families to relocate in Guelph are Pushpa Thapa's and Sarman Tamang's family. Gradually more people strating relocating to Guelph and few families imigrated directly to Guelph from Nepal. Now, more than 60 Bhutanese familes are living currently in Guelph and is expected to have reach this number closer to 100 quite soon.
